AUGMENTED REALITY - ScAnimals

ScAnimals is an engaging Augmented Reality (AR) mobile application designed to bring adorable, stylized virtual pets into the real world. By utilizing custom-designed physical marker cards (like trading cards), users can instantly spawn interactive 3D models of cats and dogs through their device camera.
